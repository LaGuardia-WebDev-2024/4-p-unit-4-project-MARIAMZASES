BUTTERFLIES

Monarch Butterfly

  • Migrating monarchs live up to 9 months
  • Monarch wings are covered in scales
  • Monarchs need one specific kind of plant-milkweed

Morpho Butteryfly

  • Their estimated lifespan is about a month
  • Their eyes are highly sensitive to UV light
  • They can tell time and track the position of the sun.

Papilio Palinurus Butterfly

  • The underside is black with orange and white spots along the hindwing edges
  • large sized butterfly
  • Lives up to 3-8 days
